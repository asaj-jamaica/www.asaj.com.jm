Jamaicans, if you want to swim for Jamaica, you must be a current member of the Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica. It's easy!

 

Swimmers wishing to compete in a competition sanctioned by FINA, the ASAJ or another Swimming Federation, must be registered with an Association recognized by FINA. Foreign swimmers wishing to compete in a Jamaican Competition must be registered by a FINA affiliated Member Federation (FINA GR3). 

 

In Jamaica, registration is not required to compete in a Development or Schools Competition. When registering, a swimmer may associate themselves to a Member Club or as Unattached.

 

If you wish to register with the ASAJ, submit a completed Registration Application Form, a copy of your birth certificate and the application fee to the ASAJ Office (ASAJ Bylaw BL6 and Procedure BLP8.1). You may associate yourself with a club or simply register "unattached".
