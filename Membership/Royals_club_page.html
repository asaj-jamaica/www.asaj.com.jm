The Royal Swim CLub was formed on April 1st, 1998 with 12 members. The club is striving for:
  1. Strong competitive swimmers.
  2. Disiplined swimmers, both mentally and socially.
  3. Swimmers confident in their ability, accepting that losing sometimes is no failure and that the only real failure is giving up.
