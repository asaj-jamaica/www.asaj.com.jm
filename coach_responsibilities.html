Coaches Responsibilities

 Head Coach
  • To maintain overall responsibility for the National Squad and National Junior Squad programs.
  • To develop a training plan for the National Squads and National Teams selected for competition.
  • To retain day to day responsibility for the National Squad.
  • Establish and maintain communication with overseas-based swimmers.
  • To monitor swimmers training programs and academic results.
Assistant Coach
  • To retain day to day responsibility for the responsibility for the Junior National Squad.
  • To assist the Head Coach.
