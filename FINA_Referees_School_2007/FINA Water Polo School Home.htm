NOVEMBER 9-11, 2007 - MONTEGO BAY, JAMAICA

JAMAICA TO HOST FINA WATER POLO REFEREE'S SCHOOL IN MONTEGO BAY

 
  October 14, 2007 - Due to construction delays at the Sunset Beach Resort and Spa, the FINA Water Polo Referee's School will now be held at the Iberostar Rose Hall Beach Hotel, a fantastic brand new resort located just 25 minutes east of Montego Bay. There has been a slight change is accommodation rates. Click here for the latest FINA Memo relating to the change.
  September 14, 2007 - The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica is proud to be the host of a FINA Water Polo Referee's School to be held November 9th - 11th, 2007. The School will be held at the Sunset Beach Resort and Spa located in the north resort of Montego Bay.  FINA will be distributing a bulletin about the School shortly. Single, double and family rates will be offered, so you can combine your "school work" with a fantastic family vacation. More details will be posted after FINA distributes their bulletin.

BRING YOUR FAMILIES TOO!

 September 23, 2007 - FINA has officially announced that the 2007 Water Polo Referees School for the Americas will be held in Montego Bay, Jamaica. All Federations in the Americas should have received an email from FINA with the Invitation Letter, School Program and Nomination Form.  Referees wishing to attend should send a completed nomination form to FINA by October 10th, 2007.
  September 23, 2007 - ACCOMMODATION AT THE SUNSET BEACH RESORT AND SPA - The rates published in the FINA Information Letter are ALL INCLUSIVE, except for transportation to and from the Donald Sangster Airport which is located 15 minutes from the hotel. The ASAJ School Organizing Committee will publish transportation information shortly. Federations or Referees wishing to make reservations at the Sunset Beach Resort and Spa should first contact the Organizing Committee.
