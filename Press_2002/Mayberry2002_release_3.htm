17 RECORD FALL IN MAYBERRY INVESTMENT’S PREP SWIM MEET

Home

February 8, 2002

Seventeen Meet Records fell today at the Mayberry Investments Prep Schools Swimming Championship held at the National Stadium Pool. In the closest finish in years, just 26 points separated Champions Immaculate Prep and challengers Mona Prep in the combined points standings. Immaculate scored all 386 of their points in winning the Girls Champions Trophy, but Mona Prep had a balanced effort from the male and female swimmers and won the Boys Division scoring 202 points to finish ahead of St. Peter and Paul (192). In the Primary School division, Matthew Hilton led the way by breaking 4 11-12 Meet records, and carried Excelsior Primary to the Champions podium with a total of 54 points, outscoring rivals Dunrobin Primary.

Five hundred and thirty six (536) swimmers from twenty seven (27) schools competed in the days events. The Meet continues on Saturday when the High School and University swimmers dive into action.

VENUE:                  National Stadium Pool (50M)

DATES & TIMES:       

High/University - Saturday February 9.             8:30 – 11:30 AM & 2:00 – 6:15 PM

The Pool will be open 1 1/2 hours before starting time.
