Eastern Collegiate Athletic Conference names Jamaica’s Angela Chuck as Swimmer of the Week

Home

February 8, 2002

After a week of scintillating performances in the pool, Jamaica’s Angela Chuck was named by the Eastern Collegiate Athletic Conference swimmer of the week. Chuck, last weekend led Brown University to Ivy League victories over Cornell University and Columbia University – Barnard College. A freestylist, Dawn Chuck captured three individual events and was a member of the winning 400-freestyle relay team.  Chuck broke the 50-freestyle pool record with a time of 23.80, the 100-freestyle pool record with a time of 58.48 and the 200-individual medley pool record, posting a time of 2:08.23.  Swimming the final leg of the 400-freestyle relay team, the Kingston, Jamaica native helped the team break the pool record by three seconds with a time of 3:30.92.

Chuck, who represented Jamaica at the Sydney Olympics, and is presently preparing for the Commonwealth Games, will hope for similar successes when the Brown Bears goes up against Yale University on tomorrow (February 9).
