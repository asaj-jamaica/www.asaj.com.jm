7TH ANNUAL RENEWAL OF THE ‘Y’ SPEEDOS’ KARL DALHOUSE MEMORIAL INTERNATIONAL SWIM MEET, JANUARY 25-27, 2002.  

Home

The leading local swim clubs will find new competition in the water for the 7th annual renewal of the ‘Y’ Speedos’ Karl Dalhouse Memorial International Swim Meet to be held Friday, January 25 through to Sunday, January 27, 2002, at the National Stadium Swimming Pool.

The Meet is held in memory of the Club’s founder, the late Karl Dalhouse who was tragically killed in a car accident several years ago. Each year overseas teams have participated and this year is no exception.

A team of twenty (20) swimmers from the Stingray Swim Club of Grand Cayman and two CCCAN swimmers from the Dorado de Paraiso Swim Club in the Dominican Republic will give some good competition to the three hundred and twenty swimmers from the local clubs which will be participating.

Hosts, the ‘Y’ Speedos Swim Club led by National swimmers Jason James, Todd Johnson, Kristie Irving, Lemone Lowe, Tiffany Walton along with up and coming Alford Green will be putting forward a strong challenge to win the trophy back from the Tornadoes Swim Club.

This meet is the first meet of the season in which swimmers may make qualifying times for Carifta and CISC.

The first session of the meet begins on Friday, 25th January at 5.30pm. Competition continues on Saturday at 8.30am and 4.00pm, with the final session on Sunday at 9.00am.

(Please note that the telephone lines at the stadium pool are down, contact can be made through 362 3623 during the meet.)

Barbara Briggs – Meet Co-ordinator. January 25, 2002
