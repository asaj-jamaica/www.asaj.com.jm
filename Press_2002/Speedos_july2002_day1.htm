July 25, 2002

On the first day of competition, three of Jamaica's National Swimmers were in action at the Speedo Champion Series Swim Meet at the Hall of Fame Pool in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Alia Atkinson and Allison Bardowell for Fort Lauderdale and Nicky Cain for Comets all participated in the in the 200 metre Freestyle Relay. Alia swam the leadoff leg for FLST "A" who finished 4th in 1:51.38. Allison on the "B" team, finished 11th with her swift anchor leg of 27.73 propelling  them to 1:53.49. Nicky's Comets finished 20th in 1:56.13 helped by anchor leg of 28.13

Tony Morrison
