41 SCHOOLS ENTER MAYBERRY INVESTMENTS SCHOOLS SWIM MEET

Home

February 7, 2002

One thousand & thirty three (1033) swimmers from forty-one (41) schools have entered the Mayberry Investment Schools Swimming Championships which dives into action at the National Stadium Pool tomorrow (Friday Feb. 8) at 8:30 AM. The meet, which has always enjoyed strong support, has attracted twenty-seven (27) Prep and Primary Schools, twelve (12) high schools and two (2) Universities. 

On Friday Prep and Primary Schools will be competing or top honors for three  team trophies; Top Girls, Top Boys and the Combined Championship Trophy. The High schools swimmers will dive into action on Saturday February 9 to compete for the Mayberry Champion Girls and Boys Trophies, while the University swimmers will vie for the Mayberry Champion University Trophy. Individual awards will include Medals for the three fastest swimmers in each event.

Mayberry Investments will be using the meet to educate the swimmers and parents about benefits of sound financial management. In making his companies commitment to the meet, Mayberry’s Managing Director Mr. Chris Berry said, “Mayberry Investments Limited believes in the development of our children.  We are not only committed to maximizing returns on investments, we are also interested in giving our children an opportunity to realize their potential.  We have undertaken a third year of sponsorship of this swim meet, harnessing the talent, strength and enthusiasm of our children. This meet teaches them the importance of sportsmanship, teamwork, humility, honesty, hard work and dedication. This is in tandem with qualities that we emphasize here at Mayberry Investments Limited" 

Wolmers Prep are the overall defending Champs in the Prep Division, while Immaculate Prep and Wolmers will seek to remain as champions in the female and male prep divisions respectively.

Immaculate High, who have dominated girls schools swimming in the last year, will want to continue this trend and defend their title. St Georges College is the defending Male Champions and the University of the West Indies the Universities Champion.

VENUE:                  National Stadium Pool (50M)

DATES & TIMES:       

Prep Schools - Friday February 8,  8:30 – 11:30 AM & 2:00 – 6:00 PM

High/University - Saturday February 9.             8:30 – 11:30 AM & 2:00 – 6:15 PM

The Pool will be open 1 1/2 hours before starting time.
