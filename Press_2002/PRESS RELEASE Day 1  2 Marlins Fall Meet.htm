PRESS RELEASE – TORNADOES SWIM CLUB

 

Results Of Day 1 and 2 – Marlins Fall Swim Meet, Ocala, Florida, October 18th – 21st, 2002.

 

 

The Tornadoes Swim Club after day 2 of the Marlins Fall Swim meet has a medal haul of 7 Gold, 7 Silver and 1 Bronze medals. Outstanding performances by Brad Hamilton in the 11-12 age group have him with a medal tally of 3 Gold and 1 Silver; Gold – 200yds Freestyle – 1.56.65, 50yds Butterfly – 26.73,50yds Freestyle – 24.28   and silver in the 500yds Freestyle – 5.14.85.
