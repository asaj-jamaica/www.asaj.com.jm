PRESS RELEASE – Tornadoes Swim Club – Results from Marlins Fall Swim Meet, Ocala, Florida, 2002

 

 

The Tornadoes Swim Club returned yesterday from Ocala, Florida where they competed in the Central Florida Marlins Fall Meet.  The team was very successful winning 10 Gold, 11 Silver and 4 Bronze medals.

Excellent performances by Brad Hamilton in the 11-12 age group had him winning 6 Gold and 1 Silver medal.  Overall the team performed very well in their first overseas meet of the season.
