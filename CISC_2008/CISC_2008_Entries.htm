ENTRY RULES

SWIMMING

1.      Each country may enter a maximum of two (2) competitors per individual event.

2.      There is no limit to the number of events in which an individual competitor may participate.

3.      No alternates or substitutes will be allowed in individual events.

4.      In relay events each country may enter only one team of four (4) competitors. FINA rule SW 10.12 will apply.

5.      No competitor may participate out of his/her Age Group, even if it were in an older Age Group than that of the competitor.

6.      The order of the swimmers in the relay events must be turned in to the Control Room not later than one (1) hour prior to the start of the session in which the event is to be swum.

7.      Each country may enter a maximum of 50 competitors.

8.      A competitor may participate in both Water Polo and Swimming.

WATER POLO

1.      Each country may enter no less than nine (9) and no more than thirteen (13) competitors per Category.

2.      Each country may include up to 2 competitors in an older age group in order to complete a team provided the country does not have an entry team in the lower group.

3.      The specific team roster for a given game must be in the hands of the Water Polo referee at least thirty (30) minutes prior to the scheduled start of the game.

4.      A competitor may participate in both Water Polo and Swimming.

SYNCHRONIZED SWIMMING

1.      Each country may enter two (2) solos, two (2) duets and one (1) team per age group.

2.      The team must be composed of a minimum of four (4) and a maximum of eight (8) competitors.

3.      No competitor may participate out of her Age Group, even if it were in an older Age Group than that of the competitor.
