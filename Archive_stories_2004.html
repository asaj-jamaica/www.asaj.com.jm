JULY

July 30, 2004 - The Jamaica Olympic Association has announced Jamaica's Olympic Games Swim Team. The team comprises four swimmers: Sydney veterans Janelle Atkinson and Angela Chuck and newcomers Alia Atkinson and Jevon Atkinson. They will be Coached by Jackie Walter, while John Eyre will accompany the team as Manager. The team will fly out  on Air Jamaica on Saturday August 7.
