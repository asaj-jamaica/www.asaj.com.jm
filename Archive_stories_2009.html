JULY

July 31, 2009 - Brad Hamilton establishes another national senior record on Day 6 of the FINA World Swimming Championships. He finished the 100M Butterfly in 56.56, erasing the old mark of 57.07. He had earlier swam a personal best time of 23.87 in the 50M Freestyle. Jonathan Wong also has a personal best time of 24.40 in that event. In the 50M Butterfly, Kendese Nangle was just off her personal best, finishing the race in 30.18. Jamaica will have its last swim when Natasha Moodie dives in the 50M Freestyle on Day 7....more

July 30, 2009 - Natasha Moodie had her first swim on Day 5 of the FINA World Swimming Championships, returning a personal best time of 57.71 (seed time 59.36) in the 100M Freestyle.  Jamaica will be well represented on day 6 as Brad Hamilton and Jonathan Wong will compete in the 50M Freestyle, Kendese Nangle in the 50M Butterfly and Brad Hamilton return later in the 100M Fly....more

July 29, 2009 - As world and championship records continue to fall at the 13th FINA World Swimming Championships, FINA Bureau announces new rules for swimsuits. Effective 2010, suits will be restricted to "textiles" and cannot go below the knee. Men suits cannot go above the navel while women suits cannot go above the neck or beyond the shoulders....more

July 29, 2009 - Jonathan Wong swam a personal best time of 53.35 in the 100M Freestyle on Day 4 of the FINA World Swimming Championships. In the same event, Brad Hamilton stopped the clock at 52.37. In the 50M Backstroke, Kendese Nangle finished the race in 31.53 secs. Natasha Moodie will have her first race, 100M Freestyle on Day 5....more

July 28, 2009 - Ramon Walton mined gold medal in the 50M Backstroke in the 17 - 19 age group at the Florida Gold Coast Junior Olympics held in Plantation, Florida from July 23 - 26. He also won silver in the 100M Backstroke. Dominic Walter won bronze in the 15 - 16 200M Butterfly while Victoria Ho just missed out on the medals, placing fourth in the 15 -16 200M Freestyle, 400M Freestyle and 200M IM....more

July 28, 2009 - Brad Hamilton smashed another national senior record on Day 3 of the FINA World Swimming Championships, this time in the 50M Breaststroke. He finished the race in 29.40, erasing his own previous record of 29.91. He will again be in the pool tomorrow (along with Jonathan Wong) in the 100M Freestyle. Kendese Nangle will swim in the 50M Backstroke....more

July 27, 2009 - Two Jamaicans were in action on Day 2 of the FINA World Swimming Championships. Jonathan Wong clocked 1:58.27 (seed time 2:01.81) in the 200M Freestyle and Kendese Nangle finished the 100M Backstroke in 1:08.37 (seed time 1:08.19). Brad Hamilton will be in the pool again tomorrow in the 50M Breaststroke....more

July 26, 2009 - Brad Hamilton shattered the national senior record in 50M Butterfly at the FINA World Swimming Championships. He finished the race in 25.68, bettering the old mark of 25.90. Kendese Nangle and Jonathan Wong will be in action today....more
