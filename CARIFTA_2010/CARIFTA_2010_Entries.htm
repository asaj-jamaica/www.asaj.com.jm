ENTRY RULES

SWIMMING

1.      There is a limit of 8 competitors in each sex per age group to a maximum of 36 swimmers.

2.      Each country may enter a maximum of two (2) competitors per individual event.

3.      There is no limit to the number of events in which an individual competitor may participate.

4.      No alternates or substitutes will be accepted after final entries have been submitted for individual events.

5.      In relay events each country may enter only one team of four (4) competitors (Participants must have been entered in the meet).  

6.      No competitor may participate out of his/her Age Group, even if it were in an older Age Group than that of the competitor.

7.      The order of the swimmers in the relay events must be turned in to the Control Room not later than one (1) hour prior to the start of the session in which the event is to be swum.

8.      Individual events longer than 200 Metres will be Timed Finals. All other events will have preliminary heats and one final event.

9.      The eight (8) fastest swimmers in the preliminaries will qualify for the finals. The 9th and 10th fastest in each preliminary event will be designated as alternates for the finals.

10.    Seeding for the preliminary events will be in accordance with FINA Rule SW3.

11.    Any event containing eight (8) or fewer entries will automatically be in the final.

WATER POLO

1.      Each team may enter no less than nine (9) and no more than thirteen (13) players.

2.      Each country may enter one (1) team per age group.

SYNCHRONIZED SWIMMING

1.      Each country may enter two (2) solos, two (2) duets and one (1) team per age group.

2.      The team must be composed of a minimum of four (4) and a maximum of eight (8) competitors.

3.      No competitor may participate out of her age group, even if it were in an older age group than that of the competitor.
