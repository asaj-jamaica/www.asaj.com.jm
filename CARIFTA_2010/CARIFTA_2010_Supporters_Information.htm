SUPPORTERS PACKAGES

 

The Organizing Committee is pleased to welcome the Caribbean area swimming fraternity to Jamaica, and offers a supporters package for US$125 per person if paid by March 11, and US$130 thereafter. There is a special children (ages 4-11) rate of US$100 per child. THESE PACKAGE PRICES WILL NOT BE AVAILABLE ON ARRIVAL IN JAMAICA.

Package include:

  • Round trip transportation - Airport/Hotel/Airport
  • Two daily round trips - Hotel/Pool/Hotel
  • Entry Pass for all events (Season ticket/Wrist bracelet)
  • Commemorative CARIFTA 2010 Magazine
  • Daily Heat/Score Sheets

Supporters' Registration and Payment Form (pdf format)

Registration forms for this package must be returned by March 26, 2010  with full payment.  The completed forms are to be emailed to carifta2010accom@gmail.com or faxed to (876)906 1484

ID Pass (Wrist bracelet) must be worn at all times. This bracelet will be used as your pass to board the buses and for admission to the pool.
