JANUARY 27, 2005

 

 

BRAD HAMILTON TO REPRESENT JAMAICA IN FINA SWIMMING WORLD CUP – NEW YORK FEBRUARY 11-12, 2005

 

 

Brad Hamilton, arguably Jamaica’s top all round male swimmer will become Jamaica’s first ever representative at a FINA Swimming World Cup Meet when he compete in the Event to be held in New York, February 11-12, 2005.

 

Hamilton, who currently holds eighteen (18) Individual National Age Group records and six (6) National Age Group Relay records will compete in six events during the two day meet: He will swim the 50, 100 and 200 Meter Freestyle, the 50 and 100 Meter Butterfly and the 100 M Individual Medley events.

 

Hosted by USA Swimming, the New York FINA World Cup event is the seventh in a series of eight events swum around the world. The final event will held in Rio de Janeiro, February 18-20, 2005. For more Information about the event, log on to www.usa-swimmming.org

 

Hamilton next local competition will be the Karl Dalhouse Sprint Meet being held this weekend (January 29) at the National Stadium Pool.
