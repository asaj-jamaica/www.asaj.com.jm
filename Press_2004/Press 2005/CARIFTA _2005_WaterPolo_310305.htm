Jamaica off to A winning start in carIfta water polo competition

March 31, 2006

Jamaica defeated Barbados 8-6 in an exciting game to win game one of the 2005 CARIFTA Water Polo Competition being played in Curacao. Robert Hamilton and Gwayne Ross who scored two goals each, and player of the match Marlon McIntyre with one goal, gave Jamaica an insurmountable 5-1 advantage at the half. Jamaica's other goal scorers were; Jordan Chung,  Nicholas Vaughn and Payton Bhoorasingh.

Coach Lazslo Borbely noted that although the team was a bit "rusty", he was happy with the overall performance of the team as they scored each time they had the man advantage.

The Jamaicans will next play the Netherlands Antilles at 11:15 AM on April 1.
