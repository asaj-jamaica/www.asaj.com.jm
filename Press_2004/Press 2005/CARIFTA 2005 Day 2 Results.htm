DATE: APRIL 1, 2005

 

 

BRAD HAMILTON SMASHES 16 YEAR OLD AGE GROUP RECORD

 

 

Brad Hamilton tonight smashed Andrew Phillips 16 year old 1517 age group 200 M Freestyle National Age Group Record swimming 1:58.58 to take the Bronze Medal at the CARIFTA Swimming Championship being held in Curacao. Phillips record of 2:00.08 was set in Hamburg in 1979. Cayman’s Shaune Fraser won the event with a time of 1:54.94.

 

In other events, Jason Adams took the Silver in the 11-12 50 M Butterfly swimming 29.45 while Alford Green claimed the Bronze in the 13-14 200 M Freestyle with a personal best time of 2:07.57.
