AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION OF JAMAICA

 

 

“AB” SHORT COURSE SWIM MEET DIVES INTO ACTION TODAY

 

 

October 21, 2005

 

The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica will open this season’s competitions with the staging of the “AB” Short Course Swim Meet held from Friday, October 21st to Sunday, October 23, 2005 at the National Stadium Pool.  The Meet has attracted approximately 350 swimmers from 9 Clubs –Y-Speedos, Blue Seals, Electric Eels, Dolphins, Flying Fish, Marlins, Montego Bay unattached and defending champions Tornadoes Swim Club.

 

The Meet will be used as a local qualifier for swimmers preparing for the 2006 CARIFTA Swimming Championship to be held in Barbados.  The sessions will commence as follows:

                    Friday   -   Evening session                    6.00pm

                    Saturday – Morning Session                   8:30 a.m.

                    Saturday – Afternoon Session                4:30 p.m.

                    Sunday                                                 9:00 a.m.

 

For further information, please contact Swimming National Technical Director, Jacky Walter at 330-4496.
