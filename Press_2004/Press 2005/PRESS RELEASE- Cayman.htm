PRESS RELEASE

 

TORNADOES SWIM CLUB OFF TO CAYMAN

 

 

 

A 34-member strong Tornadoes Swim Team departs for Grand Cayman on Friday, November 4, 2005 at 8:45 a.m., via Cayman Airways, to participate in the 2005 Michael Lockwood Memorial Swim Meet.  The Meet, which is being hosted by the Stingray Swim Club, will be held at the Lions Aquatic Centre, Georgetown, Grand Cayman, from November 4-6, 2005.  The team is boosted by National Development Squad members:  Nicole Chin, and Andre Smith and Junior Squad Members Mathue Duhaney, and Dominic Walter.

 

The Meet, which is part of the annual Pirates Week Celebrations, has attracted 4 Clubs, including two (2) other clubs from Jamaica.

 

Coaches Jacky Walter, Syreeta Daley and Miguel Jonas, will accompany the team and they will return to the island on Sunday, November 6, 2005 at 9:00 p.m.

 

While in Cayman, Coach Jacky Walter may be contacted at Comfort Suites (345) 945-7300.  Results will be available on Stingray’s website at  www.caymanswimming.com.
