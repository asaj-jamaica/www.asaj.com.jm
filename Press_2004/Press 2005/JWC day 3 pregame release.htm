JAMAICA FACE CROATIA IN JUNIOR WATER POLO WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS

 

October 8th, 2005

 

Jamaica will today play Croatia in their second match of the 13th FINA Junior Men’s Water Polo World Championships being held in Mar del Plata, Argentina. Match time is 14:30 local (12:30 Jamaica time). The match could well be another one sided affair as Croatia is ranked forth in the world playing the typical physical European style of water polo. Immediately following Jamaica’s game, Serbia-Montenegro will play Columbia in the other Group D match of the day.

 

Jamaica’s Coach Laszlo Borbely gave the Jamaicans the day off yesterday to recover from their first game with Serbia. The young Jamaican players, who are on an extremely steep learning curve, are determined to improve their performance with each game and will today work to reduce the number of break away and man up goals that they gave Serbia.

 

In yesterdays Group D matches, Serbia demonstrated their power once again defeating France 21-3 while Croatia beat Columbia 15-2.
