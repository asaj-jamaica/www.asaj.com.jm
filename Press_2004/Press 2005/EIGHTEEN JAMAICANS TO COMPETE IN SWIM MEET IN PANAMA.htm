EIGHTEEN JAMAICANS TO COMPETE IN SWIM MEET IN PANAMA

 

 

Four Marlins Swim Club and fourteen Y-Speedos Swim Club swimmers will travel to Panama City, Panama in a joint delegation to compete in the XIV Torneo International Delfin De Oro Swim meet to be held December 15-19, 2005.

 

Lurline Todd is the Team Manager while Y-Speedos and National Assistant Coach Lynval Lowe will travel with the team as Head Coach.
