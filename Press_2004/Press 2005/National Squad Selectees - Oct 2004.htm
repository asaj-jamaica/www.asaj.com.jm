AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION OF JAMAICA

SQUAD SELECTION – OCTOBER 2004

 

January 2, 2005 - Congratulations to the following swimmers who have been selected to the National Elite, Junior and Development Squad. Training is expected to start on Wednesday January 12 – time and venue to be advised (posted on the www.swimjamaica.com).

 

We apologies for the delay in starting training but we promise that the plans being prepared for 2005 will be exciting for everyone. Before the start of the January 12 training session, a meeting with the swimmers, their personal coaches and parents will be held (1 hour). At this time the swimmers, personal coaches and parents will be required to sign a commitment form;
