JAMAICA DRAWN IN GROUP D FOR FINA JUNIOR MEN’S WATER POLO WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS

 

 

July 14, 2005…..Jamaica’s preliminary round opponents in the 13th FINA Junior Men’s Water Polo World Championships are now known after FINA completed the draw yesterday in Montreal. The Championships will be played on Mar del Plata October 7th-15th, 2005. Jamaica’s Junior Men’s Team, who have qualified for the World Championships for the first time, will first play Serbia-Montenegro on the opening day of competition. Croatia, France, Iran and regional opponents Colombia will be Jamaica’s other opponents in the preliminary round.

 

The twenty four (24) teams playing in the Junior Water Polo World Championships will compete in four (4) groups, with each team playing five (5) games in the preliminary round. The top sixteen teams will move onto the final round.

 

The Jamaican Team is currently in training at the National Stadium Pool. While they are fully focused on the World Championships, the Jamaicans are keen to test their readiness when they compete in the Central America and Caribbean Swimming Championships which will be held in Santo Domingo, August 1-9.
