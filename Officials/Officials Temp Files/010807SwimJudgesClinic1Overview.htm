SWIM JUDGES CLINIC TO BE HELD JANAURY 13, 2007

 

January 8th, 2007

 

Thank you for volunteering to be a swimming judge. Every role you play, no matter how small, is critical to the sport and the development of our children and young athletes. We appreciate your time and effort.

 

The first of a series of three clinics will be held at the Air Wing Audio Visual Room (Up Park Camp) on January 13, 2007 (2:00 - 4:00 PM). Please be on-time! All three clinics are important if you want to develop a good all round knowledge. The attached agenda describes what we will cover in this clinic. Clinic 2 will deal with the description and application of the stroke rules. Clinic 3 will deal will On Deck Procedures, Starter and Referee issues as well as a review before CARIFTA.

 

You will note on the calendar that every clinic has been scheduled just before a competition - we want you to do the classroom time and then have the opportunity to practice what you have learnt into practice on-deck (please volunteer to act as a judge at those meets).

 

We want the clinic to be very interactive so it would be helpful if you read the FINA Rules, 1, 2, 3, 11, 12 & 13 before the clinic. You may also find it helpful to review some of the information contained on our TECHNICAL OFFICIAL section of www.swimjamaica.com
