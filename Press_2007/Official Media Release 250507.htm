25 May, 2007

 

World Masters Championships 2008 Qualifying Times Released

 

The Organising Committee today published the XII FINA World Masters Championships 2008 qualifying times revised by FINA.

 

According to Marketing Manager John Lynch the times will see a lot more competitors qualify for events.

 

“FINA have taken into account a much broader skill level and this will be good news for many who are swimming close to the old marks. There is no doubt these times will allow many more swimmers to participate.”

 

The event will run from 15th -25th April 2008 in Perth, Western Australia.

 

Competitors can find out more information or register for the event by going to: http://www.2008finamasters.org/
