JAMAICANS ALIA ATKINSON AND NATASHA MOODIE STAR AT ART ADAMSON INVITATIONAL

November 24, 2007

 

At the recent Art Adamson Invitational hosted by Texas A and M University Alia Atkinson a student had two individual wins in the 100 and 200 Yard Breastroke races in NCAA automatic qualifying times. She took the 100 Breastroke in 1:01.03 and the 200 Breastroke in 2:12.93. She also scored victories as a part of the A Team in the 200 and 400 Yard medley relays.

 

In the both relays she had the fastest breastroke splits of 27.97 and 1:00.76 respectively. Both relay team times were NCAA consideration times of 1:40.07 and 3:38.46 respectively. She also had consideration times in the 200 Yard Individual Medley (2:03.22) and the 100 Yard Butterfly (54.63). In the last event of the day Atkinson had two consideration times in one race as the leadoff swimmer for the 400 Free Relay C team swimming 50.33 (consideration time for the individual event) and the relay team made the standard finishing in 3:23.70.

 

Compatriot and University of Michigan swimmer won the C Final of the 100 free in 52.17. She later went faster leading off the B team when stopped the clock in a fast 51.32. The format of the meet had heats in a 50 meter pool and finals in a 25 yard pool.
