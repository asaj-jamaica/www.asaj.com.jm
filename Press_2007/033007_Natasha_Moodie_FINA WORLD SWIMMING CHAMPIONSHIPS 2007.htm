FINA WORLD SWIMMING CHAMPIONSHIPS 2007

 

MELBOURNE, AUSTRALIA

 

MARCH 25TH TO APRIL 1ST,2007

 

 

March 30, 2007….Melbourne, Australia.

 

Jamaica’s lone representative at the FINA World Swimming Championships Natasha Moodie has established 2 personal best times in the four events to date.

 

Her best performance to date was in the 100m Freestyle where she placed 3rd in her heat, and was touched out by the 1st and 2nd place in the last 5 meters.  Her results up to March 30 are;

 

100m Breaststroke   placed 51st     1.19.02           PB      

200m Freestyle         placed 72nd    2.12.26

100m Freestyle         placed 56th     58.65              PB

50m Butterfly             placed 61st     30.74

 

Natasha has her pet event the 50m Freestyle on Saturday, March 31, where she will swim out of lane 8 in heat 13 of 18 heats.
