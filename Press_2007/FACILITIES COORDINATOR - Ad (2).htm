FACILITIES COORDINATOR

 

The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica seeks to identify suitable candidates to fill the position of Facilities Coordinator for the National Stadium Pool complex and the Bournmouth Bath (to be opened later this summer). The ideal candidate would have already had proven experience in facilities management and should additionally be able to display good people and communication skills. Additionally, she/he would have been trained at the tertiary level and should possess the ability to relate effectively to personnel at all levels of the organization. The successful candidate will be sent on a Pool Management Course.

The Core objectives for and duties associated with this position are to manage all the activities at the physical plant of the National Stadium Pool including maintaining oversight of the gate and rental operations of the facility so as to maximize revenue, minimize cost and provide the best possible customer service. This would be achieved mainly,

       By developing and maintaining control over a suitable maintenance program for the swimming pools, buildings, sanitary facilities and its environs to meet standards as defined by the ASAJ.

       By developing, implementing and coordinating appropriate usage guidelines and schedules for users of the facility,

       By effective management of the maintenance budgets, as well as gate and other revenue operations.

Persons interested in filling this position should submit an application including a  curriculum vitae by Friday June 8, 2007 to:
