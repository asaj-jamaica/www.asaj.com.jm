GILLIAN MILLWOOD POSTS 4 TOP EIGHT SWIMS

 

December 2nd, 2007


Gillian Millwood a Jamaican sophmore at Hiram College posted 4 top eight swims at the Longnecker Invitational held in Pennsylvania. Millwood a 2005 CARIFTA finalist in the 100 and 200 metre butterfly events first helped Hiram B to eigth spot in the 200 yard freestyle relay with her relay lead off split of 26.77 (30.73 long course metres) for a total time of 1:47.34.  

 

In the 400 yard medley she again helped the B team to eight position 4:32.24 with her anchor freestyle leg of 57.65 (1:05.96). In the 200 yard medley she was on the A team and put that team fifth 2:00.25 with a third leg butterfly split of 28.91 (32.96). In the 100 yard butterfly she stopped the clock in 1:04.56 to grab sixth place. She finished ninth in the 50 yard freestyle in 26.55(30.73).
