TORNADOES SWIMMERS OFF TO CHATTANOOGA, TENNESEE

 

 

January 17, 2007

An all-male delegation of ten (10) swimmers from the Tornadoes Swim Club will depart on Thursday 18 January 2007 for Chattanooga, Tennessee to compete in the Baylor Swim Club TYR Invitational Swim Meet which will be held from  Friday, 19 January  to Sunday 21 January 2007.  The members of the team are:   Jason Adams, Stefan Brown, Matthew Chen, Daniel Daley, Brian Forte, Brian Graham, Richard Graham, Sean Saunders, Andre Smith and Dominic Walter.  Also competing at the Meet will be their teammate, Brad Hamilton, who now attends the Baylor High School, the host team.

 

This Meet will be another opportunity for the swimmers to qualify for the CARIFTA Games in April and the Central American and Caribbean (CAC) Swimming Championships in July.  Results will be available online at http://www.baylorswimming.org.  Coach Jacky Walter will accompany the team.
