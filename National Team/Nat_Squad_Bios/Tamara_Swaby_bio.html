Tamara Swaby

 
Date of Birth: December 6,1978

Height: 5’ 91/2 ” 

Weight: 142 lbs. 

College: Oakland University 

Swim Club: Y Speedos Swim Club

 

 

2001-2002 SWIMMING GOALS: Make NCAA and finish my degree.

ACHIEVEMENTS IN 2001:

  • Jamaican CCCAN Team Member, second in 50 Free, life time best times in 100 m (1:04.97) and 200 m (2:23.74) Butterfly, 50 Free.

  • Qualified for US Open.

  • 2001 Mid Continent Athlete of the year.

  • NCAA B time qualifier.

  • Nominated for NCAA athlete of the year for Michigan.

  • Made 2001 Mid continent all team conference.

ACHIEVEMENTS IN 2002:

  • Selected to Jamaica's 2002 Commonwealth Games Swim Team

INTERNATIONAL ACHIEVEMENTS: 

  • Represented Jamaica at CARIFTA

  • Represented Jamaica at Caribbean Island Swimming Championships

  • Represented Jamaica at Central American and Caribbean Swimming Championships.

  • Represented Jamaica at the 1999 Pan American Games

  • Mid-Continent athlete of the year for 2000, Mid-Continent athlete of the month December 2000,

  • Golden Grizzly of the week,

  • Given Mid Continent All American Academics. Tamara is also the captain of the Golden Grizzly Swim Team.

HOBBIES: swimming, reading, dancing, and hanging out with my friends.

FAVORITE SUBJECTS: Organizational Behavior

PETS: None at this time, but she loves cats.
