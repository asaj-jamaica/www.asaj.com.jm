Janelle Anya Monique Atkinson

 

Date of Birth: Sept.  30, 1982   

Height: 5’ 5”

Weight: 137 lbs.

College: University of Florida

High School: Campions College & Bolles School

Club: Gator Swim Club/ Bolles Sharks

 

 

 

2001-2002 SWIMMING GOALS: Medal at Commonwealth Games and NCAAs.  Also to try and break some school records and Jamaican National records.

FIRST REPRESENTED JAMAICA: CISC 1994

JA. REPRESENTATIVE AT:       

  • CARIFTA

  • CCCAN

  • CISC

  • CAC

  • Commonwealth Games

  • Pan American Games and

  • Sydney 2000 Olympic Games.

MAJOR ACHIEVEMENTS:  

  • 3 Silver Medals – Pan American Games August 1999

  • Gained Olympic qualification for 400 M Freestyle and 800 M Freestyle at Pan American Games August 1999

  • Placed 4th in 400 M Freestyle at Sydney Olympics 2000

  • Set a new Commonwealth Records for the 800 SCM Freestyle (8:20.13) at the 2001 US Open

OTHER ACHIEVEMENTS:      

  • National Youth Awardee 1999 – Government of Jamaica

  • Female High School Swimmer of the Year 1999 – Swimming World Magazine, U.S.A.

  • World Ranked 400 M and 800 M Freestyle

HOBBIES: listening to music, watching TV and reading
