Selection of National Squads
The National Team Selection Committee will meet on the last Wednesday of February and September December with the following minimum agenda items:
  • Review previous Training Forms and school reports (if applicable).
  • Review swimmers performances for previous term.
  • Select National Squads for subsequent term based on Selection Criteria.
The Chairperson of the Selection Committee will submit the Committee's decisions to Council for ratification.
