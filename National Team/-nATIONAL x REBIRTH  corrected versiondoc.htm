National X – Re-Birth

 

To qualify for the National X program swimmers must be 13 yrs and above and a member of the Junior or Senior National Squads.

To remain in the program the swimmer must be able to fulfill the following criteria

1.              Attend 10 training sessions per week or as agreed for CXC and ‘A’ Level swimmers.

2.              Be on time for all sessions, consideration will be given to individuals who finish school late.

3.              Willing to work hard at all sessions
