Training Schedule for May to June, 2008

Printable Version  (PDF)

 
December 6, 2010 - The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica has named swimmers to its national senior, junior and development squads....list

 
April 26, 2010 - National Squad training sessions are scheduled for Wednesday afternoon (4/28 4:30 - 6:00) and Saturday afternoon (5/1 3:00 - 4:30). All swimmers should attend one session as test sets will be done to establish training groups for the next cycle of training.

 
June 3, 2008 - National Senior/Junior squad and 2008 CARIFTA/CISC team members are invited to a clinic on June 13-14 to be conducted by Bob Emary of Broward Aquatic Swim Club....more

 
April 30, 2008 - The training calendar for the national squads for May and June is now available....schedule

 
January 16, 2008 - The training calendar for all national squads for the period January to April, 2008 is now available....schedule.

 
December 17, 2007 - The schedule for the National Squad Camp on December 27-30, 2007 is now available. The camp will be conducted by Coach Rob Emary. Click here for details.

 
September 24, 2007 - The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica today named sixty-nine (69) swimmers to its national senior, junior and development squads....press release.....list

 
December 26, 2006 - The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica for the 2nd time will be hosting Coach Rob Emary of Broward Aquatics Swim Club in Fort Lauderdale who with with the assistance of local coaches, will be running a Swim Camp for National Squad Swimmers from Wednesday December 27th to Saturday December 30th....press release

 
November 29, 2006 - Jamaica's National Senior Squad Swimmers Ramon James, Matthew Hilton and Dominique Dawson leave tomorrow to compete in the 2006 Holiday Classic Invitational Swim Meet hosted by the Gator Swim Club at the University of Florida, Gainesville

 
October 18, 2006 - Two swimmers have been added to the National Squad. Ramon James qualified for the Senior Squad with a 1:00.72 at the Summer Age Group Champs (somehow his time was not presented by Team Manager - we apologize Ramon). Rohan Johnson, who attends school in the USA, qualified for the Junior Squad swimming 50.13 in the 100 yards Freestyle (58.90 LCM converted). Congratulations to both swimmers....revised list

 
October 18, 2006 - Congratulations to all the swimmers who have been offered a place on one of the ASAJ's National Squads....click here for a list of the 54 swimmers

 
May 18, 2006 - As we prepare for CISC and the CAC Games, National Coach Jackie Walter has restarted the National "X" training programme....more

 
April 13, 2006 - National Squad Grade Sheets are to be handed into the ASAJ Office before Wednesday May 31, 2006

 
April 13, 2006 - National Squad Commitment forms are to be handed into the ASAJ Office before Wednesday April 19th, 2006.

 
February 22, 2006 - Rick Bishop will lead Jamaica's National Squad Swim Camp to be held March 2-4, 2006. Watch out for more details

 
February 22, 2006 - Jamaica's 2006 CARIFTA Team and the National Squad Selections will be announced after a special Council Meeting to be held this evening.

 
January 4, 2006 - There will be a meeting with Technical Director Jackie Walter for all coaches with swimmers on the National Squad on Wednesday January 5th, starting at 5:30 PM at the National Stadium Pool....coaches are asked to bring their attendance sheets for December 05

REGISTERED SWIMMERS

SUBMIT YOUR MEET RESULTS HERE

Your meet results are very important to us. Keeping us informed of your swims, ensures that all of your times will be included in the ASAJ Database, and will be used for National Squad, National Team and Jamaican Top Time consideration. When you complete this form, your results will be emailed directly to the ASAJ Office. The ASAJ will send an email confirming the we have received your times.
