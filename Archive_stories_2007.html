May 31, 2007 - The ASAJ is seeking a suitable candidate to fill the position of Facilities Coordinator to manage the National Stadium Pool and Bournemouth Bath which will be reopened later this summer....more

May 30, 2007 - The ASAJ, SwimJamaica, The Kingston Rotary Club and the Combined Disabilities Association have teamed up to renovate and operate the Bournemouth Bath multi purpose Facilities located on the Kingston waterfront. The ASAJ will be responsible for Managing and Maintaining the two pools (one eight lane 25 M pool and a shallower learn to swim pool) while SwimJamaica will be the primary Learn to Swim program at the facility. The group is seeking JA$5 million more to close the renovation budget - persons or companies who are interested in contributing to this community development project should contact the ASAJ President John Eyre (920-6230)

FEBRUARY

February 27, 2007 - Brad Hamilton Leads Baylor School To State Championship. Brad Hamilton had an outstanding meet at the 2007 Tennessee State High School Swimming Championships in Nashville, Tennessee at the Tracy

Caulkins Aquatic Center. Brad won the 50 freestyle and 100 freestyle in times of 20.70 and 45.15. Both of these times were with in hundredths of a second off of the Tennessee state record. Brad combined with teammates Greg Roop, Michael Vredeveld, and Jake Hora to win the 200 freestyle relay in a new school and state record time of 1:24.69. In the 400 freestyle relay, Brad, Greg, Nathan Vredeveld, and Josh Sosna combined to win the event in a new state record time of 3:08.30.

 

The Baylor School won the combined team championships, and won the boys team championship for a record 19th time, but it's first win since 1997. Baylor Head Coach Dan Flack was voted Mens Coach of the Year.
