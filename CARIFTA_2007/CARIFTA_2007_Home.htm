CARIFTA NEWS

April 8, 2007 - After two days of exciting competition, in which twenty seven meet records were broken (12 on day 1 and 15 on day 2), the French Antilles leads from Bahamas and Trinidad.

April 4, 2007 - The first 151 persons (swimmers, officials and supporters) from 3 participating countries arrived today. The teams here are from Bermuda, Bahamas and French Guyana.

April 2, 2007 - 263 swimmers from 13 Caribbean Nations have entered the 2007 CARIFTA Swimming championships....more

April 1, 2007 - CARIFTA Team Managers can now view the lunch options available....more (PDF)

March 31, 2007 - The Psych Sheets for the Swimming Championships have been added....more

March 22, 2007 - 248 swimmers from 13 Caribbean Countries have entered the 2007 CARIFTA Swimming Championships. The ASAJ will today officially announce that the competition will be hosted by Jamaica at the national Stadium Pool.

February 13, 2007 - The details of the 2007 CARIFTA Supporters Package being offered by local hotels and the Organizing Committee has been posted to the Supporters Information page....more

February 13, 2007 - Air Jamaica, the Official Carrier of the 2007 CARIFTA Swimming Championships is offering a discounted rate on air travel for team and supporters traveling to Jamaica for the Championships....more

January 8, 2007 - The CARIFTA 2006 Congress Minutes are complete. Click here to download the Minutes and the latest version of the CARIFTA Guidelines.

December 25, 2006 - The CARIFTA 2007 Organizing Committee hope that you will all have a Merry Christmas and a fantastic 2007 - we look forward to seeing you all in Jamaica in April.

November 22, 2006 - A number of corrections have been made to the 2007 CARIFTA Swimming and Synchronized Swimming Summons, please ensure that you use the latest version dated November 22, 2006.

November 21, 2006 - Welcome to the new CARIFTA 2007 Home Page. Click here to see the Meet Summons. The Organizing Committee is working with Air Jamaica to offer special CARIFTA 2007 Group Fares - we should have these available within two weeks. We will also publish our supporters packages shortly.
