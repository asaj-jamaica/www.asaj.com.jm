SUPPORTERS PACKAGES

The 2007 CARIFTA Swimming Championships Organizing Committee is offering the following supporters packages. Please note the payment deadlines hotel accomodation.

2007 CARIFTA Supporters Package Rates (PDF)

2007 CARIFTA Supporters Package Registration & Payment Form (PDF)

Air Jamaica air travel discount offer (PDF)

The Supporters’ Package includes:

  • Round trip transportation – Airport/Hotel/Airport
  • Two daily round trips to and from the pool from designated departure points
  • Entry Pass for all events and Closing Ceremony (Season ticket/Wrist bracelet)
  • Commemorative Magazine
  • Daily Heat Sheets

ID Pass (Wrist bracelet) must be worn at all times. This bracelet will be used as your pass to board the buses, admission to the pool which will include heat/finals sheets.

PRICE: USD125.00 per person

Supporters taking advantage of this package are requested to return their Registration form to the ASAJ offices by March 12th 2007 with a 50% deposit

All monies must be paid to the ASAJ - See Supporter package registration form for payment details.

Hotel Accommodation:

We have secured special rates at selected hotels for the supporters. The hotels are as follows:

  • Hotel Four Seasons  -  www.hotel-four-seasons-jam.com
  • Courtleigh Hotel & Suites – www.courtleigh.com
  • Hilton Hotel – www.hiltoncaribbean.com/kingston/
  • The Jamaica Pegasus – www.jamaicapegasus.com

All hotels require that you book your reservations directly with them, submitting your room lists at the time for booking. Please note that each hotel has specific booking and payment deadlines

Deadlines:

30 days prior to arrival – accommodation deposits due at the following hotels:

        • Courtleigh Hotel & Suites
        • Hilton Hotel
        • The Jamaica Pegasus

March 12th - Reservations deadline for the Hilton Hotel

                     Supporters registration form to be faxed/emailed to the ASAJ and deposit

                      paid.
