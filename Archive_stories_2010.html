January 1, 2010 - The Members of the ASAJ Council wishes for everyone in the Jamaican Aquatics Community a happy and prosperous 2010. The year will no doubt be challenging, but we are confident that with everyone playing a role, we can together, make the ASAJ a better organization.

It is our hope that 2010 will be seen as a turning point for the Aquatic Sports. If this is to happen we must work:

1. to change the structure of the ASAJ so as to encourage increased participation, particularly in parishes outside Kingston;

2. to make each Technical Committee a robust and effective development tool;

3. to secure additional sources of funding for local competitions and National Teams;

4. to continue the rapid expansion of SwimJamaica;

5. with the Government and other organizations to secure funding for new aquatic facilities;

6. to ensure that the new G.C. Foster Aquatics Programme flourishes;

7. to ensure that CARIFTA is a success, in all respects.

We look forward to working with you to achieve these and other goals and pray that 2010 be a good one for you and your family.
