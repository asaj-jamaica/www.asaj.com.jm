ASAJ - FINANCE AND AUDIT COMMITTEE
OPERATIONAL FRAMEWORK
Name of Committee: Finance and Audit Committee
Scope of Responsibility: Budgets, Fundraising and Statutory Reporting.
Committee Objectives:
1. To develop and control the expenditure budgets of the Association.
2. To plan, organize and control the Association funding activities.
3. To develop operational policies for executing the financial functions of the Association.
Description of Duties:
1. Coordinate the development of the Association's annual and medium term budgets as well as the funding and control of such budgets within the approved limits of Council.
2. Receive and review meets and other committee budgets and approve expenditure within the prescribed guidelines.
3. Arrange for and receive all gate receipt from fundraising activities..
4. Establish and monitor all internal guidelines fir the operation of the Association's expenditure.
5. Provide a written report at each Council meeting on the financial operations of the company and highlight for discussion or approval significant items and/or those failing to conform to the Association's internal standards.
6. Prepare and execute on the Association's behalf all necessary statutory returns related to the Association's financial operations.
Procedural Guidelines:
1. The Committee chairperson shall be a member of Council. The Chairperson shall communicate with committee members to keep them fully informed.
2. The Committee shall adhere to the policy direction of Council.
3. The Committee shall adhere to the rules of the ASAJ and FINA.
4. Advise Council on matters referred to the committee for consideration.
5. Make recommendations to the Council on any matter that concerns the carrying out of the Committees duties.
6. The Committee Chairperson shall submit to the ASAJ Secretary, for filing, a copy of all relevant documents and correspondence.
7. The Committee Chairperson shall submit to the ASAJ Secretary, for filing, a copy of all relevant documents and correspondence.
8. The quorum for Committee meetings shall be three persons.
9. The Committee may, with the consent of Council, co-opt persons to assist in particular projects or programmes.
10. Report to each monthly Council meeting, the progress of the committee's duties.
