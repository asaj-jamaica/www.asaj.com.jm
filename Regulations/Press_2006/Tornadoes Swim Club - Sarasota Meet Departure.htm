FOURTEEN TORNADOES SWIMMERS TO COMPETE IN THE

2006 Kent B. Holdren Memorial Swim Meet

 

Headed by National Representatives Dominic Lee, Steven Moodie, Richard Graham and Dominic Walter, a 14-member delegation of the Tornadoes Swim Club leaves the island on Thursday, May 18, 2006 to compete in the Kent B. Holdren Memorial Swim Meet hosted by the Sarasota YMCA Sharks at the Selby Aquatics Centre, May 19-21, 2006 in Sarasota, Florida, The Meet has attracted approximately 400 swimmers.

 

The National Representatives will be using this Meet to continue their training for the upcoming Caribbean Island Swimming Championships (CISC) to be held in Puerto Rico, June 26-July 1, 2006. Other team members include:

 

Head Coach, Jacky Walter along with Coach Kathy Lim-Sang will accompany the team. Results will be available on www.sysharks.org. The team returns to the island on Tuesday, May 23, 2006.
