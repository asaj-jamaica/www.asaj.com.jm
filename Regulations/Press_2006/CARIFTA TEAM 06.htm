JAMAICA CARIFTA 2006 TEAM

 

February 24, 2006

 

The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica (ASAJ) has announced the thirty two (32) swimmers, eighteen (18) male and fifteen (15) female water polo players to represent Jamaica at the 2006 CARIFTA Swimming Championships to be held in Barbados April 6-9. The number of water polo players will be reduced to the official team size of 13 male and 13 female players on March 5th when the final entries have to be submitted to the organizers in Barbados.

 

Sixteen swimmers qualified for the team using the AA Time standards used for CARIFTA Selection and after long discussion, the ASAJ Council, decided to modify the selection criteria in an attempt to maximize points by ensuring relay team in all age groups. In addition, Council also agreed to allow the Selection Committee swimmers to consider swimmers who would qualify in one event with an AA Time.

 

The CARIFTA Organizers have announced that the CARIFTA Synchronized Swimming Competition will not be held in Barbados due to insufficient entries. Efforts are being made to find another date and host country.

 

Congratulations to all.
