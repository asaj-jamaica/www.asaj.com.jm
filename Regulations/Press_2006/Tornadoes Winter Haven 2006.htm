TORNADOES SWIM CLUB

 

PRESS RELEASE

 

 

STINGRAYS WINTER HAVEN SWIM MEET 2006

 

A 16-member delegation of the Tornadoes Swim Club leaves the island on Thursday, April 20, 2006 to compete in the Winter Haven Stingrays 2006 “Season Opener” to be held April 21 – 23, 2005 in Winter Haven, Florida.  A total of 465 swimmers from 25 clubs will compete in the Meet.

 

The team comprises CARIFTA Swimming Championship representatives Matthew Chen, Richard Graham and Dominic Walter.  Tornadoes Swim Club Head Coach, Jackie Walter, will accompany them.   Results will be available on www.midnightsports.com.  The team returns to the island on Monday, April 24, 2006.

 

 

While in Florida, Coach Jackie Walter can be contacted at 305- 301-7032, for further details.
