ASAJ HOSTS CAMP FOR CARIFTA SWIM TEAM

 

The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica will be hosting a camp for members of the 2006 Carifta Team and newly selected National Squads from Thursday March 2nd to Saturday March 4th 2006.

 

The camp will be conducted by Coach Rick Bishop head coach at the Baylor High School in Chatanooga, Tennessee, USA.  Coach Bishop was coach for Janelle Atkinson at the 1999 Pan Am Games, and presently coaches Stephanie Napier the top high school sprinter in the USA for 2005-06.

 

The sessions will be on

Thursday March 2nd       4-6pm

Friday March 3rd              5-7am

Friday March 3rd              3-5pm

Saturday March 4th        5.30-8.30am

Saturday March 4th        3-5pm

 

There will also be a session for coaches on Friday March 3rd from 11-12pm and parents of swimmers on the squads on Friday March 3rd from 5.15-6.15pm.
