Overseas Guest Coach for Y Speedos - February 15 – 25

 

 

February 14, 2006

 

‘Y’ Speedos Swim Club welcomes Dianne Roestel who is visiting Jamaica for the first time and has volunteered to work with the Y Speedos Swim Club during her vacation time on the Island.  During her stay she will share her skills and knowledge with “Y” Speedos Age Group coaches and will be involved in specialist training with the senior age group swimmers

 

Dianne Roestel is the Head Age Group Coach for Nose Creek Swim Association in Calgary, Canada and Head Coach for Fast Lane Competitive Swim Camps (fastlaneswinimming.ca). She is a certified level three Coach.  She is a highly motivated individual with excellent coaching skills and great work ethics. She has a passion for fitness and has experience training swim clubs in Canada and the USA.

 

Welcome Dianne to Jamaica!!
