MARCH 2, 2006

 

JAMAICA’S TODD JOHNSON SHINES FOR HAMILTON COLLEGE

 

Many times National representative Todd Johnson performed outstandingly last weekend at the New England Small College Athletic Conference Championships. Representing Hamilton College, Todd placed 3rd in the 100 yard Backstroke in 51.30 and 4th in the 200 yard Backstroke in 1.53.17 which qualified him for the NCAA Division 3 Championships to be held from March 15-17, 2006.

 

He also placed 5th in the 50 yard Backstroke in 24.90 having gone 24.50 in the preliminaries to establish a Hamilton College record.
