April 11, 2006

 

ADTECH RENEWS SPONSORSHIP OF THE INTER-SCHOOLS SYNCHRONIZED SWIMMING CLASSIC

 

The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica (ASAJ) will be hosting the 3rd ADTECH Inter-Schools Synchronized Swimming Classic at the National Stadium Pool April 21st and 22nd, 2006. ADtech is an organization committed to providing the best combination of technology, flexibility and cost effectiveness in products and solutions.  ADtech remains a faithful sponsor of the Inter Schools Synchronized Swimming Classic for three consecutive years, making it the highest level school synchronized swimming competition for the year.

 

Synchronized Swimming is a graceful sport that involves strength, endurance and flexibility with exceptional breath control while underwater. Several corporate area institutions will be competing for the ADTech Trophy at this annual event. In a continuous battle to maintain the winning title, St. Andrew, defending champions, will be facing intense competition from schools such as Wolmers, Immaculate, Campion and other tertiary level schools as they build their creativity and technique.

  

The figures competition will take place on Friday, April 21st and the exciting routines will be performed on Saturday, April 22nd where each school will enter solos, duets and team pieces.
