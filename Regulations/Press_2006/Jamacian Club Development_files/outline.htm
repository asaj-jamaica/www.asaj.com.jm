Involving the Media
  • Cultivate a relationship with local sports writer
    • Explain the game
    • Define virtues of your club (e.g., providing children the opportunity to realize their dreams, the Olympic Ideal, out reach programs, etc.)
    • Invite to games and escort
    • Human interest stories
    • Stay in touch with your zone media rep or USA Water Polo media
    • Be persistent!

17
Economic Factors
  • Create Scholarship programs
    • Work with business, local government and other agencies to support youth sport - don’t leave anyone out of the opportunity
  • Create managable roles for parents
    • Single parents have to focus on work - help educate them on the benefits of sport in their children’s lives
    • Help parents share responibilities in keeping kids engaged
      • Car pooling
      • Event support
    • If necessary hold several club educational sessions to accommodate parents’ schedules

Creating a Volunteer Network
  • Make the jobs managable and meaningful
    • Don’t overwhelm your volunteers
    • Have clear objectives
  • Reward volunteers
    • Community recoginition
    • Employer recognition
    • Annual Awards
  • Teach Parents how important volunteers are to their childerens’ future both in and out of sport
