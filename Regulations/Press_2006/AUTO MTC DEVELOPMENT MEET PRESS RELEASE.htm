MARCH 6TH 2006

 

AUTO MAINTENANCE LTD. SPONSORS MARCH 11 DEVELOPMENT SWIM MEET

 

THE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION OF JAMAICA (ASAJ) TODAY ANNOUNCED THAT AUTO MAINTENANCE LTD., A COMPANY OFFERING COLLISION REPAIRS AND AUTOMOTIVE SERVICING, WILL SPONSOR THE AUTO MAINTENANCE DEVELOPMENT SWIM MEET TO BE HELD AT THE NATIONAL STADIUM POOL ON MARCH 11.  THIS IS THE SECOND DEVELOPMENT SWIM MEET TO BE HELD IN 2006.

 

THE MEET, WHICH HAS 26 EVENTS, WILL BE HELD IN THE 25 METRE NATIONAL STADIUM POOL WITH ENTRIES LIMITED TO TWO HUNDRED AND FIFTY (250) SWIMMERS. THE DEVELOPMENT SERIES OF MEETS HAS BEEN DEVELOPED TO PROVIDE CLUBS AND SCHOOLS A TOOL TO DEVELOP THEIR YOUNGER AND MORE INEXPERIENCED SWIMMERS.

 

ALTHOUGH THE PRIMARY FOCUS OF THE MEET WILL BE DEVELOPMENT, THE 2006 CARIFTA SWIMMING CHAMPIONSHIPS ARE JUST ONE MONTH AWAY AND NATIONAL COACH JACKIE WALTER HAS ENTERED HER NATIONAL TEAM SWIMMERS IN THE 50 METRE EVENTS . THE TEAM HAS JUST COMPLETED THEIR POST SELECTION SWIM CAMP.

 

AUTO MAINTENANCE LTD. IS LOCATED AT 15-17 ST. JAMES AVE, OFF MAXFIELD AVENUE KINGSTON 5.
