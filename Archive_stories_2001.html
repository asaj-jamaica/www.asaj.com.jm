September 21, 2001 - NATIONAL SQUAD QUALIFIERS NAMED Thirty-nine swimmers have attained the qualiying times for and have been invited to the National Swim Squad. The ASAJ Council on Sept 13, 2001 ratified the recomendations submitted by the Selection Commitee. Congratulation to everyone selected - lets start working hard for next years four big competitions...

2001-2004 MEET QUALIFYING TIMES APPROVED The ASAJ Council has approved the Meet Qualifying Times for all International Competitions up to and including the 2004 Olympic Games. The times for the 2003 Pan American Games are provisional as ASUA, who has oversight over the games, has not yet published their qualifying times. The criteria for calculating the qualifying time was developed to create a progression of faster times from CARIFTA to the Olympic Games.
