New...April 9, 2003 - A total of 13 countries have submitted final entries for the CARIFTA Swimming Championships which will dive into action next week Friday evening with two Water Polo Games. All thirteen will compete in Swimming, three in Synchronized Swimming, three in the male Water Polo competition and two in the female Water Polo competition....list of countries

April 1, 2003 - The Supporters Accommodation Information Package has been updated to include The Courtleigh, a sister hotel to the Knutsford being used at the Games Village. The Courtleigh is located on Knutsford Blvd. in New Kingston

March 15, 2003 - FINA has approved the 2003 CARIFTA Swimming Championships and the 2004 Caribbean Island Swimming Championships as qualifying competitions for the 2004 Olympic Games.

Feb. 9, 2003...Invited Federations (see below) are reminded that Preliminary Entries for CARIFTA 2003 are due on February 12, 2003....see the latest tally

 

Air Jamaica has offered a 15% discount on travel to Jamaica for the Championships....see details of Air Jamaica offer
