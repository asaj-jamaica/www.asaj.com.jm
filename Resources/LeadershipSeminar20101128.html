INVITATION

LEADERSHIP TRAINING SEMINAR

AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION OF JAMAICA

4:00 PM TO 6:00 PM - NOVEMBER 28th, 2010

JAMAICA OLYMPIC ASSOCIATION, 9 CUNNINGHAM AVENUE

 

The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica (ASAJ) will be hosting a LeadershipTraining Seminar on Sunday November 28th, 2010 (4:00 PM to 6:00 PM) at the JOA Training Room, 9 Cunningham Avenue.

OVERVIEW

Volunteer leaders drive the development of sport worldwide. This seminar is one in a series of education opportunities that the ASAJ will provide for current or aspiring leaders of the aquatic sports. We invite our member Club Leaders, ASAJ Council Members and ASAJ Committee Members as well as other interested members to attend.

COST: No charge

REGISTRATION: 3:45 – 4:00 PM November 28th, 2010

AGENDA
