1000 SWIMMERS ENTER MAYBERRY INVESTMENTS SCHOOLS SWIM MEET

Home

Mayberry Investments Limited has renewed it’s strong relationship with Jamaican swimming announcing that it will sponsor the Mayberry investments schools swim meet  to be held at the National Stadium Pool, Friday February 14 and Saturday February 15, 2003.  1000 swimmers are from 43 schools have entered the competition.

While schools will be competing or top honors in four divisions; Prep School, Primary School, High School and Universities, the 17 and Over High School and Universities swimmers will race each other to add excitement to the meet. Medals will be awarded to the three fastest swimmers in each event. Special prizes will also be handed out. Mayberry Investments will be using the meet to educate the swimmers and parents about benefits of sound financial management.

In making his companies commitment to the meet, Mayberry’s Managing Director Mr. Chris Berry said, “Mayberry Investments Limited believes in the development of our children.  We are not only committed to maximizing returns on investments, we are also interested in giving our children an opportunity to realize their potential.  We have undertaken a third year of sponsorship of this swim meet, harnessing the talent, strength and enthusiasm of our children. This meet teaches them the importance of sportsmanship, teamwork, humility, honesty, hard work and dedication. This is in tandem with qualities that we emphasize here at Mayberry Investments Limited”.

Immaculate Prep are the overall defending Champs in the Prep Division, while Immaculate Prep and Mona will seek to remain as champions in the female and male prep divisions respectively.

Immaculate High, who have dominated girls schools swimming for some time, will want to continue this trend and defend their title. Campion College are the defending Male Champions and the University of the West Indies the Universities Champion.

VENUE:                  National Stadium Pool (50M)

DATES & TIMES:    Friday February 14,              8:30 AM (Prep) 2:00 PM (Prep)

                              Saturday February 15.          8:30 AM (High) 2:00 PM (High)

                              Pool will be open 1 hr. before starting time.
