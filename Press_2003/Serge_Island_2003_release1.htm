SERGE ISLAND DAIRIES SPONSORS AGE GROUP SWIM CHAMPIONSHIPS

Home

FEBRUARY 26, 2003

SERGE ISLAND DAIRIES YESTERDAY ANNOUNCED THEIR SPONSORSHIP OF THE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION’S (ASAJ) SPRING AGE GROUP SWIMMING CHAMPIONSHIPS TO BE HELD AT THE NATIONAL STADIUM POOL, FEBRUARY 27-MARCH 2, 2003. 441 SWIMMERS FROM NINE (9) CLUBS, AN INCREASE OF 45 SWIMMERS OVER 2002, HAVE SUBMITTED ENTRIES FOR THE CHAMPIONSHIPS.

THE SERGE ISLAND DAIRIES AGE GROUP SWIMMING CHAMPIONSHIPS, WHICH IS ONE OF THE PREMIER EVENTS ON THE SWIMMING CALENDAR, EXPECTED TO GENERATE MANY EXCITING PERFORMANCES AS THE TOP AGE GROUP SWIMMERS COMPETE TO SECURE A SPOT ON JAMAICA’S 2003 CARIFTA SWIM TEAM WHICH WILL BE SELECTED NEXT WEEK. AN EXCITING NEW FEATURE OF THIS YEARS COMPETITION IS THE ADDITION OF EVENTS IN ALL FOUR STROKES FOR MASTERS SWIMMERS 25 YEARS AND OLDER. SWIMMERS FROM 4 YEARS TO 63 YEARS OLD ARE ENTERED AND WILL COMPETE TEN (10) SERGE ISLAND DAIRIES TROPHIES.

AN ELATED ASAJ PRESIDENT JOHN EYRE THANKED SERGE ISLAND FOR DEMONSTRATING THEIR COMMITMENT TO JAMAICANS OF ALL AGES.  THE SERGE ISLAND DAIRIES AGE GROUP SWIMMING CHAMPIONSHIPS WILL DIVE INTO ACTION ON THURSDAY FEBRUARY 27 AT 5:30 PM.
