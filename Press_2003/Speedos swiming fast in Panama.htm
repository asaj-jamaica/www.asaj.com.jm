RAYNAE HALL AND MATTHEW HILTON STRIKE GOLD IN PANAMA

 

 

December 14, 2003……After three days of swimming against 32 teams from 21 countries 'Y' Speedos are in 12th place in the X111 International Delfin De Oro Swim Meet in Panama City.

 

The medal tally is led by 8 & Under girl Raynae Hall who has won three gold medals in the 50m Freestyle, 50m Backstroke and the 50m Butterfly, she has one silver in the 200m Individual medley and a bronze medal in the 50m Breaststroke. Matthew Hilton follows closely with a gold medal in the 11-12 boys 100m Breaststroke in a personal best time of 1:15.11, a silver medal in

the 50m Freestyle and a bronze in the 100m Butterfly. 8 & under boy Corey Richards swam a personal best time to win a silver medal in the 50m freestyle.

 

With a medal tally of 4 gold, 3 silver and 2 bronze medals the ten member team continues in action tomorrow - Monday, and are due to return home on Thursday after a two day rest in Panama along with Coach Lynval Lowe and Manager Stewart Spencer.
