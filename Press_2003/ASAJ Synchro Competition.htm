INTER SCHOOLS SYNCHRONIZED SWIMMING COMPETITION

 

March 4, 2004……The Amateur Swimming Association of Jamaica will be staging its first Inter Schools Synchronized Swimming Competition March 5 & 6, 2004 at the National Stadium Pool.  Four (4) Schools are slated to compete in this inaugural event.  They are Campion College, Immaculate Conception High School, St. Andrew and Wolmer’s High Schools for Girls.

 

The competition will be staged in two (2) parts:

 

1.               The Figures Competition (Technical Skills) which will be on Friday, March 5, 2004 commencing at 4:30 p.m.

 

2.         The Routine Competition (Performance Aspect) which will be on Saturday, March 6, 2004 commencing at 3:30 p.m.

 

 

The competitors will vie for points in Tier 1 to Tier 6 depending on their skill level.  There will be solos, duets and team performances at each Tier Level.  A trophy will be awarded to the winning school.
