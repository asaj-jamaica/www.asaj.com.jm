JASON ADAMS WINS 9 GOLD, 2 SILVER AND 2 BRONZE IN CAYMAN

Home

November 3, 2003

The Tornadoes Swim Club returned to the island, from the Mike Lockwood Memorial Swim Meet held in Grand Cayman form Oct 30th to Nov 2nd, 2003, with a medal haul of 24 Gold, 27 Silver and 24 Bronze medals.

Jason Adams swimming in the 11-12 age group was the outstanding performer winning 9 Gold, 2 Silver and 2 Bronze medals.  High point trophies were won by Megan DeLeon in the 9-10 girls, Dominic Walter 9-10 boys and Brian Forte 11-12 boys.

The competition was very exciting with a number of swimmers doing personal best times.  The club placed second with 24 swimmers, a very creditable performance.
