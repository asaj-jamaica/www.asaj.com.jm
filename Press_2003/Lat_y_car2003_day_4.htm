September 6, 2003  
      
The 50 metre Breastroke saw another closely contested event with the Jamaicans actually leading the pack for the first 25 metres but fading towards the end. Barbadian Lon Clarke (34.94) outsprinted Linval Lowe (35.47) in the final stretch, pushing him into third while Brian Van Romondt of Aruba  (39.19) accelerated in the last 5 metres to pass Bair (40.26) still on the comeback trail for 6th.
 

In the 100 metre Freestyle "Tony" Morrison was up against Cliff Gittens former Barbadian national record holder in the 50 metre event. It was close going at the halfway mark with "Tony" turning at 26.91 but a bad turn made it too much to ask and with Gittens finishing well it was Gittens with the Gold in 55.25 and Morrison-Vincent second in 58.75, a season best. Brazilain Ricado Santos won his age group title in the second fastest time of the day of 55.15

Submitted by Tony Morrison.
