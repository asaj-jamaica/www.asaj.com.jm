March 17, 2003

The ASAJ has named fifteen female and sixteen male swimmers to the Water Polo Male and Female Squads preparing for the CARIFTA 2003 Swimming Championships which will be held in Jamaica April 19-21. The Squad which is now in training at the National Stadium Pool will be reduced to the maximum required thirteen for each team at the end of March.
