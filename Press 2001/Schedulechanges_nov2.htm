ASAJ POSPONES PREP SCHOOLS MEET

Home

Persistant rains has forced the ASAJ to postpone the ASAJ Pres Schools Swim Meet until November 17, 2001. The competition session times will remain the same. Over 400 swimmers from 16 schools had enter the competition. The postponement will mean the schools may make changes to their entries which must be submitted to the ASAJ office no later than 4:00 PM on Thursday November 8. Schools still wishing to enter the meet but who failed to do so by the original deadline may do so, providing the entries are submitted by the new entry deadline. 

The Timekeepers Clinic originally scheduled for November 17 has been moved to November 10th (2:00PM - 5:00 PM). Persons wiching to participate may contact the ASAJ office for more information.

November 2, 2001
