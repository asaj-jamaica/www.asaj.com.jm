TRINIDAD'S BOVELL 4TH IN 200 M IM

Trinidad and Tobago,s George Bovell swam the race of his life when is finished 4th in the finals of the 200 M Individual Medley at the 9th FINA World Swimming Championships being held in Fukuoka, Japan. Bovell who's previous best time was 2:04.51 just missed the bronze medal and a chance to stand on the awards podium as he touched a mere .59 second behind Australia's Justin Norris (2:00.91). The event was won by Italy's Massimiliano Rosolino who finished in 1:59.71 while America's Thomas Wilkens took the silver (2:00.73).
