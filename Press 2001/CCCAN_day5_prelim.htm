DOMINIC LEE BREAKS JAMAICAN 13-14 200 M BREASTSTROKE AGE GROUP RECORD AT THE CCCAN.

Dominic Lee broke the Jamaican National Age Group Record for the 13-14 200 M Breaststroke by swimming 2:35.03 in the preliminary heats on day 4 of the Central American and Caribbean Swimming Championships in Santo Domingo. Lee’s time qualified him for the final with the fastest time.

The following Jamaican swimmers have qualified for the finals:
