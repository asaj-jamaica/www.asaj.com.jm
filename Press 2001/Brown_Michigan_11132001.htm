ANGELA CHUCK POWERS BROWN UNIVERSITY BEARS OVER UNIVERSITY OF MICHIGAN
November 13, 2001. The following was taken from the Brown University website.

The women's swimming and diving team hosted Big Ten Power Michigan on Saturday, October 27, in Smith Swim Center in a scrimmage. In front of a very large and supportive crowd, the Bears were victorious in eight events as they prepared for their first meet at Harvard on November 16.

Winners for the Bears were:

Senior Angela Chuck, junior Laura Mosca, senior Judy Koonstra, and sophomore Elezabeth Daniels in the 200 Medley Relay.

Sophomore Jean Lee in the 1650 and 500 Free.

Angela Chuck in the 200 and 100 Free.

Daniels in the 50 Free.

Koonstra in the 200 Fly.

Daniels, junior Inbal Hasbani, Koonstra, and Chuck in the 400 Free Relay.
