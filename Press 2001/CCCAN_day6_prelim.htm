Eight Swimmers Qualify For Finals On Final Day Of CCCAN Championships

In the final preliminary heats session of the Central American and Caribbean Swimming Championships, all but one of the Jamaican swimmers who swam qualified for the finals. Six of the nine swims were best times.

The following Jamaican swimmers have qualified for the finals:
11-12 400 M Freestyle             M      John-Claude Wright     4:58.28                9th

15-17 100 M Backstroke           M      Jevon Atkinson          1:03.32              8th (PB)

13-14 100 M Backstroke           F       Allison Bardowell        1:12.29                8th (PB)

                                          M      Todd Johnson            1:04.93              5th (PB)

11-12 200 M Butterfly             M      Travis Forte               2:34.28              6th (PB)

18 & Over 50 M Free              F       Angela Chuck             27. 50                1st

                                                   Tamara Swaby           28.00                3rd

                                          M      Ramon James             24.47                5th (PB)

Janelle Atkinson will swim the final of the 18 and Over 400 M Freestyle. No preliminary heat was required as there were only eight entries.

Results of swimmers not making the finals:
11-12 200 M Butterfly                   M       Brad Hamilton     2:53.15           13th (PB)      

 

Finals starts at 17:00 local (16:00 Jamaican time)
