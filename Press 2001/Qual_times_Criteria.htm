The following criteria was used to establish the proposed ASAJ International Competition Qualifying times:

  1. CARIFTA (all age groups):         USS AA Standards.
  2. CISC (all age groups):                 USS AAA Standards.
  3. CCCAN (11-12, 13-14):            USS AAAA Standards.
  4. CCCAN (15-17, 18 & Over):     Except for 50m and 100m Freestyle, half way between USS AAA and AAAA Standards. All qualifying times made finals in the 2001 CCCAN.
  5. CAC and Pan Am Games – To be confirmed (ASUA has not yet published the 2003 qualifying times). On ASAJ spreadsheet, 1999 times have been used for ASAJ Male qual. Times. As most ASAU times for the Females were slower that the ASAJ CCCAN Qualifiying times, USS AAAA Standard or 1999 ASAU Times, which ever was faster was used.
  6. Commonwealth Games – halfway between the 1999 Pan Am “A” Qualifying times and the 2004 Olympic Games “B” times.
  7. World Champs (50M) – FINA 2004 Olympic Games “B” standard ("A" Standard plus 5%)
  8. Olympic Games – FINA 2004 Olympic Games “A” & “B” time standards (16th place in the 2000 World Rankings).
