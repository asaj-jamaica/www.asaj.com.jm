Y’ SPEEDOS CONTINUE THEIR MEDAL HAUL IN THE DELFINES D’ORO SWIM MEET, PANAMA.

Home

The 11-12 Girls 400m Medley Relay team was the Gold medal winner Sunday, whilst Matthew Williams won two silver medals in the 7-8 Boys 200 IM in 3:16.73 and the 50m Butterfly in 39.05. Lemone Lowe in the 13-14 Boys 100m Breaststroke continued his good form by winning a silver medal in 1:17.63. Ramon Walton won a bronze in the 50m Butterfly in 35.08 with gold medallist team mate Matthew Hilton coming 4th in the same event in 36.47.

The medal tally now stands at nine gold, seven silver and five bronze.

The meet ends tomorrow, Monday, and hopes are high for another good day in the pool.

December 16, 2001 Barbara P. Briggs.
